About Robert Bridda

Robert is a Treasure Hunter. He lives in Washington Township, NJ and spends his time finding valuable items to collect and sell. His main focus is to acquire precious minerals and other collectibles such as gold, natural gems, old watches, stamps, coins, and many others. After making connections and gaining more experience looking for the right items to sell, what started as a hobby in 2020, has become his full-time job.
Fitness and Health have become part of his way of living. He is inspired to take his passion into helping others with their health goals, by starting a healing house in the future. For now, he does his best at taking care of his fitness and eating healthy as well as helping his family and friends get rid of bad eating habits.

His love for health is tied to his love for farming. Before becoming his own boss, Robert has worked in farms taking care of animals. This has made him passionate about farming, growing foods, and taking care of many animals he grew to love. When possible, he makes traveling part of his job, as well as part of his hobbies. He grew his passion by traveling to Arizona and discovering more about rock hounding when he was younger.
Robert’s best friend is named “Skipper”! He is an Australian Shepherd dog that was rescued by Robert when he least expected. They are quite inseparable nowadays. Robert’s inspirations for the future come from the bond he experienced with others, and nature. His life goals were made to help those around him, as well as in the past or present.
