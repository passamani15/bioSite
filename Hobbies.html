Hobbies

Robert loves to Treasure Hunt! He has found really interesting items in the past years. “I look for yard sales and places where people may look like they’re hoarding stuff. I see them collecting treasures of their own. I usually go to those places and almost always end up finding something worth the trip. These include old watches, jewelry, collectible items, branded clothes, coins, silverware, and many others.” - Robert Bridda

He has found his main inspirations through traveling. While doing so, he discovered interesting items that he could acquire and sell such as rocks and minerals. One of his favorite states is Arizona due to their diveresity of minerals that can be found in the desert.

This is Robert’s first meeting with his dog SKIPPER! Rescuing his dog was one of the best accomplishments he has done. Robert takes Skipper on many journeys during his work or travels. “I found Skipper on a rescue website while looking at dogs online. I was originally only thinking about the possibility of getting a dog but wasn’t fully ready to commit. I found myself really liking his picture and wanted to give it a try and see how I felt seeing him in person. It was love at first sight! I went back home with the regret of not taking him. I eventually counted every minute to pick him up the next day. This was the best decision I have ever made. We are best friends and I take him everywhere I go.” - Robert Bridda
