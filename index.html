Life by Robert Bridda

“My goal is to become financially free to invest and travel and find more treasured things in life. My ultimate career goal would be to settle on a farm as a chiropractor. Living and providing a healing house for people to visit and help them, retain youth and longevity with herbal and orthopedic remedies.”- Robert Bridda

Fitness and Health have been a major aspect of Robert’s journey. He dedicates a big part of his time trying to help others practice a healthier lyfestyle (including himself). His family plays an important part in his motivation.

Robert hopes to have a farm of his own after years of experience working at farms. He gained his experience on taking care of animals as well as the agricultural side of farming. This has also grown on his love for animals, and the cows surely think that HE IS A CATCH!
